Backstory:

While (most of) the projects in this portfolio were made from college onwards, I've been making art since I could literally remember.

From making little flipbooks on post-it note pads (before I knew they were called flipbooks), to starting my first YouTube channel when I was only 8 years old, I've always liked to make and create. My original plan was to be a music composer, and I worked towards that all throughout my childhood and high school career. When I applied for the music school at DePaul University, I realized I'd be locked into working on classical or jazz music with my trombone. So I changed my major and pivoted over to graphic design, chosen based off of my year or two tinkering with Adobe Photoshop at the time, and well, here we are!
